Ways to Help

There are lots of ways you can help. Here are my two suggestions for how you should get involved.

Learn More!

"What is Children’s Mental Health?"

Read this article written by the CDC that talks about children mental health. Most people don't really notice but children can also have mental health issues. It's important to notice if your child has mental health issues and to get them help if they do have them. We don’t really think about kids having mental health issues but it’s actually pretty common. They say if children are scared or worried about things they usually act out. Some common mental disorders children have are ADHD, anxiety, and other behavior disorders.

Depression by National Institute of Mental Health

Here is a great article that covers the basics of depression. It addresses what depression is, signs and symptoms of depression, and different types of depression. It's a great read if you're new to depression.

"Key Findings: U.S. Children with Diagnosed Anxiety and Depression”

Read this article written by the CDC that gives shocking findings about U.S Children with anxiety and depression. There is a lot of surprising facts that is in this article. For example, "more than 1 in 20, or 2.6 million, U.S. children aged 6-17 years had current anxiety or depression that had previously been diagnosed by a healthcare provider".
